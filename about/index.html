About Me

I’m a Machine Learning Engineer at Manifold - a company that builds data products and production ML systems for large and startup companies across multiple industries.

Before Manifold, I spent a year getting my MS in Data Science at the University of San Francisco and built computer vision pipelines for segmenting brain ventricles in MRI and CT scans at UCSF Radiation Oncology.

I graduated from the University of Virginia with a BS in Biomedical Engineering and after graduating I joined a cancer genetics startup and did software engineering for 2 years. I got very interested in Machine Learning and decided to get an MS in Data Science.

I write code mainly in Python and mostly have been doing machine learning, data engineering, and backend development. Occasionally, I’ve also built frontends with React.

I occasionally tweet about tech and random things. If you want to say hi, have any questions or feedback you can reach me at anishdalal1@gmail.com
